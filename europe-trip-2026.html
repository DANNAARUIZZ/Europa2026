Florence

Jun 25–27 · 2 nights
Must do today: Book the Uffizi and Accademia the moment you get tickets. June sells out weeks ahead. Both are essential — the David is one of the most moving things you'll ever see. Rome

Jun 27–30 · 3 nights
The three pastas of Rome: Cacio e pepe, carbonara, amatriciana. Eat all three before you leave. The rule: walk at least 5-10 minutes from the Colosseum, Trevi, Pantheon, and Vatican before eating — tourist-adjacent restaurants are overpriced and mediocre. Trastevere neighborhood = best food in Rome.
